# Branding & Brand Package Tools
Curated from `website_index.md` + selective additions

This document lists tools and AI projects useful for creating, designing, and maintaining a cohesive brand package, including logos, typography, color systems, naming, visual assets, and brand guidelines.

---

## 1. Naming, Identity & Brand Discovery

### Name & Username Availability
- **Namechk**  
  Checks availability of brand names and usernames across domains and social platforms.

- **Instant Username Search**  
  Fast username availability checks across major platforms.

- **BrandSnag**  
  Brand name discovery + domain and social handle availability.

### Brand Ideation
- **Namelix**  
  AI-powered brand and business name generator with logo suggestions.

---

## 2. Logo Design & Visual Identity

### AI Logo & Identity Generators
- **Looka**  
  AI logo generator with full brand kits (logos, colors, fonts, mockups).

- **LogoAI**  
  Generates logos and brand identity systems using AI.

- **Brandmark**  
  AI-driven logo creation with typography and color systems.

### Vector & Logo Editing
- **Boxy SVG**  
  Web-based SVG editor suitable for refining logos and icons.

- **SVGOMG**  
  Optimize SVG logos for web delivery.

## 3. Color Systems & Typography

### Color Palette Tools
- **Coolors**  
  Generate and explore color palettes for brand systems.

- **Khroma**  
  AI color generator trained on your preferences.

- **Color Hunt**  
  Curated color palettes for inspiration.

### Typography Tools
- **Fontjoy**  
  AI font pairing for brand typography.

- **The League of Movable Type**  
  Open-source, high-quality fonts suitable for branding.

- **Google Fonts**  
  Widely used open font library for brand systems.

## 4. Design Systems, UI & Brand Application

  Open-source design and prototyping tool for brand systems.

  Record terminal sessions for brand storytelling and demos.

## Notes
- This list emphasizes **indie-friendly, AI-assisted, and design-system-compatible tools**.
- Many tools pair well with a **terminal-themed or hacker-aesthetic brand**, especially when combined with Markdown, SVG, and web-terminal libraries.
- The strongest brand packages typically combine:
  - Naming + availability tools
  - Logo & color system generators
  - Typography pairing
  - A documented brand guideline (Markdown or Notion)
